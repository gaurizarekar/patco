About Us

Company Over View

We Providing Best Indutrial Solution For Our Customers Business Growing In Last 35 Years.

Since its inception in 1989, Patco has been at the forefront of Manufacturing Small Precision Components. With an unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we have established ourselves as a leading name in the industry.

Our 35 years of involvement in automotive precision parts and specialty in Fuel Injection & Auto parts has enabled us to become a demanding name in this business. With so many years of experience, we have attained an in depth understanding of advance manufacturing processes & procedures. Our Modern production measures enables us to make PRECISION auto parts at a large scale & reduce the unit cost and pass the same to our customers.

Patco consists of 5 manufacturing facilities spanning a total area of 250,000 square feet.

All plants are strategically located in close vicinity providing excellent logistic support.

Our success can be attributed to several key factors

  • Excellent & Latest Infrastructural Facility
    Equipped with the latest technology and machinery, we ensure that our manufacturing processes are efficient, precise, and capable of meeting the most demanding requirements.
  • Young & Well-Experienced Staff
    Our team is the backbone of our success. We have a harmonious blend of young and dynamic professionals along with seasoned experts who bring with them years of experience in the field.
  • 35 Years of Rich Experience in Manufacturing Precision Parts
    With over three decades of experience in the industry, we have honed our skills and mastered the art of manufacturing precision parts. Our expertise covers a wide range of materials, including Ferrous, Stainless Steel & Non-Ferrous, ensuring that we cater to diverse industrial needs.
  • Extensive CNC Machine Capabilities
    Our manufacturing capabilities are further strengthened by our impressive lineup of 200 Swiss, German, Japanese, & Korean CNC machines. These advanced machines enable us to handle complex projects with precision and efficiency.
  • Latest Inspection Facility for Quality Assurance
    Quality is paramount to us, and to ensure that every product leaving our facility meets the highest standards, we have invested in a modern inspection facility. Our in-process and PDI inspections guarantee that our products are free from defects and adhere to the strictest quality norms.
  • Planned & Spacious Workplace
    Our company operates from a planned and spacious workplace that is designed to optimize productivity and ensure a comfortable working environment for our employees. Moreover, this setup allows for future expansion to accommodate our growing operations and client demands.
  • Customer-Centric Approach
    When we receive an order, we treat it as an opportunity to showcase our expertise and dedication to excellence. We go above and beyond to ensure that our customers receive the best products and services in the field. Your satisfaction is our ultimate goal.
